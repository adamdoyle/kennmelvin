Clear Wolf Striking Pins

This short clip shows a clear plastic Ebonite Werewolf striking. You can 'clearly' see the weight block as the ball moves through the pins. Notice how the ball hits slightly high and the four pin is the last to fall - "tripped" by two pin messenger which has bounced off the wall. Compare this with the video where the four-pin remains standing.

This video clip will take a few minutes to load: when it does you'll see a close up of the weight block as it rolls into the pocket for a strike. There are links to other video clips at the bottom of this page.
