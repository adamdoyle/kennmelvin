NITBF Rankings: 2000-2001

Ranking Events are organised by the NITBF to provide competition for members of the Federation and to enable the selection of squads from which teams can picked for international tournaments.

Events are held on a monthly basis, with a minimum number of events having to be played for eligibility for a place on the international squad. At the end of a twelve month period offers are made to the top athletes. This page will have news of recent events and up-to-date rankings.

Ranking Events are organised by the NITBF to provide competition for members of the Federation and to enable the selection of squads from which teams can picked for international tournaments. Events are held on a monthly basis, with a minimum number of events having to be played for eligibility for a place on the international squad. To allow for holidays, work etc. players are allowed to drop their two lowest events, thus final squad offers are based on points from eight out of ten events. At the end of a twelve month period offers are made to the top athletes.
