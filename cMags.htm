  • Bowling This Month BTM (Monthly, US)
    The magazine of choice for the serious bowler. Contains technical ball reviews, detailed ball layout articles. Regular contributors are Dr. Eric Lasser (Team USA sports pyschologist), Palmer Fallgren (Team USA head coach), Jeff Briggs (Physical fitness instructor), Bill Hall, Susie Minshew and others notable in the field of bowling.
  • Go Tenpin, (Monthly, UK)
    Starting to become quite a good magazine, can be read by the complete beginner to the more serious tournament bowler. Nowhere as technical as BTM (but then noone else is!). Maintains a comprehensive UK bowling web site.
  • Bowlers Journal, (Monthly, US)
    Aimed more at the bowling centre proprietor than the casual bowler.
  • Bowling Digest, (Bi-Monthly, US)
    Regular writers include Parker Bohn, Jerri Edwards, Bill Spigner and John Jowdy
  • Asian Bowling Digest (Malaysia)
    Bowling news for the far east and Australia, including the Asian Bowling Tour.
