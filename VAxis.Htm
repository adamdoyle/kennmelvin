Clear Wolf Weight Block

This short clip shows a clear plastic Ebonite Werewolf. The Weight Block is indicated by a white line. You can clearly see the weight block rotating around the axis then levelling out as the ball hooks on the back end and rolls into the pin.

This video clip will take a few minutes to load: when it does you'll see the weight block moving inside the clear plastic ball as it hooks into the pocket. Looking inside this ball shows how the weight block is rolling, by watching the outside of the ball you can see how certain drillings allow the weight block to make a bowling ball flare, watch how the axis rotates as the ball hooks or see a close up of a strong strike.
