Lynn Black

based in Brunswick Superbowl, Derry

In her 2nd year on the squad Lynn finished 3rd in the 1999 rankings, played in the 1999 British Nationals (Plymouth) and represented Northern Ireland in the 1999 Triple Crown (Dublin).

Karen Payne

based in Dundonald International Icebowl, Belfast

Karen has represented Northern Ireland at the highest level, including the 1997 Europeans (Nottingham), the 1998 and 1999 Triple Crowns, the AMF World Cup, 1998 (Kobe, Japan) as well as the 1999 British Nationals (Plymouth).
