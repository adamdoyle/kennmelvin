Bowling Score Computer

Do you enjoy bowling but get confused by how to keep score? It's really quite simple and can enhance your enjoyment when you go bowling. You can play an imaginary game here and - at the same time - teach yourself how to keep score for any possible combination of balls. Have Fun!

Your Bowling Scoresheet

You can learn how to keep score by using the simple "computer" below. Choose your skill level (the advanced level will throw more strikes, letting you see how strings of strikes raise the score), hit the PLAY button then try to guess what the score will be: press the SCORE button to check your score against the computer. If you didn't get it right you can check it frame by frame to see where you went wrong.
