High-Flare Bowling Ball Pictures

Everyone knows that the modern bowling balls "flare", as the internal dynamics of the core (dependant upon drilling of course) cause the bowlers initial PAP to migrate along an arc towards the grip line.

As the ball flares a new oil track is created (allowing more reactive resin to make contact with the lane) and a bow-tie pattern is created. For those that are interested we have some images of a double bow-tie, which creates some mathematically intriguing moire patterns.
