Clear Wolf Striking Pins

This short clip shows a clear plastic Ebonite Werewolf striking. You can 'clearly' see the weight block as the ball moves through the pins. Notice how the ball pin 'sticks' to the head pin (using it to plough through the five, eight and nine pins) and how the two pin bounces off the wall to create the mix that carries any pins left standing.
