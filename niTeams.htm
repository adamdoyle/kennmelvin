International Teams

Membership of the Northern Ireland Internationals Squads in based on a twelve month season of Ranking Events. At the end of the season the Mens & Ladies squads are formed.

Junior and Senior squads are also formed. Squad training sessions are held on a regular basis and the National Coaches are available for individual coaching. Prior to an event a process of team selection is carried out, with input from the coach and final ratification by a selection panel. Selection Criteria include such factors as the athlete's current average (in ranking events), physical fitness, training logs, technical knowledge, equipment suitability etc.
