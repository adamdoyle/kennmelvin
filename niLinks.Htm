Some other useful sites

Below you'll find a short list of sites, not directly related to bowling but which provide information that can be of use to a serious athlete or coach.

  • Bowling in Northern Ireland, affiliated site with pictures, videos and articles to watch and download
  • Tenpin Bowling All Over the Web the single best listing of bowling web sites.
  • FIQ, Federation Internationale des Quilleurs, worldwide governing body for tenpin bowling
  • FIQ-EZ, good resource for European Bowling
  • NCF, The National Coaching Federation
